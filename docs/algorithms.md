# Algorithms

CaveGenLabs currently includes the following procedural cave generation algorithms.

Each algorithm and their parameters can be explored interactively through the application.

---

## **Random Walk** <span class="algorithm-difficulty" data-difficulty="Difficulty: ★☆☆☆☆"></span>

Carves paths through a solid grid using one or more randomly moving walkers.

**Best suited for**

- Winding tunnel systems
- Learning procedural generation fundamentals
 
---

## **Midpoint Displacement** <span class="algorithm-difficulty" data-difficulty="Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆"></span>

Generates cave passages using recursive subdivision.

**Best suited for**

- Irregular cave passages
- Connecting cave systems

---

## **Cellular Automata** <span class="algorithm-difficulty" data-difficulty="Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆"></span>

Generates organic cave layouts by repeatdely updating cells based on their neighbors.

**Best suited for**

- Natural cave systems
- Large interconnected caverns

---

## **Perlin Worms** <span class="algorithm-difficulty" data-difficulty="Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆"></span>

Generates cave systems using Perlin noise.

**Best suited for**

- Smooth winding tunnels
- Connected cave networks

---

## **Binary Space Partitioning** <span class="algorithm-difficulty" data-difficulty="Difficulty: ★★★☆☆"></span>

Generates cave rooms by recursively dividing the given area into sections.

**Best suited for**

- Dungeon layouts
- Structured room generation

---

## **Metaballs** <span class="algorithm-difficulty" data-difficulty="Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆"></span>

Carves organic, blobby caves by summing the influence of randomly placed metaballs.

**Best suited for**

- Large open caverns
- Organic cave formations

---

## **Diffusion Limited Aggregation** <span class="algorithm-difficulty" data-difficulty="Difficulty: ★★★☆☆"></span>

Creates branching caves by simulating randomly moving particles that attach to a structure.

**Best suited for**

- Branching cave systems
- Natural growth-like structures

---
